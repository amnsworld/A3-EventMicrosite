The City of Brampton's Garba

Mayor Brown and Members of Council invite everyone to take part in the City of Brampton’s first ever Garba!.This event features a roster of live entertainment, including headliner Ravin Naik, an award-winning singer, composer and musician who has toured internationally and performed at Gujarati festivals around the world. Local talent Darshana Thaker, a classically trained singer who runs the Swaraagini School of Music in Brampton, will open for Naik. Jaymin Thakkar, a former TV news anchor in Gujarat, and current radio show host with RedFM Canada, will emcee.​

Location CAA Centre - 7575 Kennedy Rd S.

Event Date Friday, - October 18, 2019

Start Time - 7:00 PM

Fees - $ 20

Event Contact/Host City of Brampton
